Research

Children's thinking about belief

Collaborators: (Mary) Shaocong Ma, Laura Soter, Pearl Han Li

In this ongoing project we examine how children and adults reason about coercive and epistemic influence on belief, in the United States and China. We ask whether these influences make belief change possible, likley, and good or bad, as well as how it might influence behavior predicitions.

Private versus Asserted Beliefs

Collaborators: Laura Soter, Pearl Han Li In this new project we ask whether judgments of a belief are influenced by whether the belief is communicated, or simply thought. We are exploring this between domains.
